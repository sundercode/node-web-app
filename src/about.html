What's Envelopost?

This is project Envelopost. We make greeting cards. This is the home of the senior capstone project for 6 University of Portland students. Envelopost is made by David Rodden, Elise Sunderland, Mitchell Estes, Nick Huey, Rachel Smith, and Scott Rowland. This idea is the spawned creation of University of Portland student and E-Scholar Katie Heitkemper.
